**Prologue – The Beginning**  
The player wakes up in a dark alley with no memories of who they are or how they got there. As they walk forward, they see a glowing library at the end of the alley that seems to be the only source of light in the darkness. Drawn to it, they enter and find endless shelves of books inside. Soon, a customer arrives without explanation, and one of the books begins to glow. The player instinctively opens it and helps the customer face their unfinished story and decide how it should end. After the customer leaves, the player notices a strange flickering book on the shelf that no one seems to claim.

**Chapter 1 – The Lost**  
Another customer arrives, this time carrying deep regret and emotional pain. Their story feels heavier and more personal, forcing the player to make more careful choices when guiding them through their memories. As the player continues helping different customers, they slowly begin to understand that their role in the library is to help people complete their unfinished stories so they can move on. The flickering book remains in the background, growing more noticeable each night.

**Chapter 2 – The Familiar Stranger**  
A new customer enters the library and reacts strangely when they see the player, saying that they feel familiar as if they have met before. This confuses the player and makes them question their identity. The glowing book connected to this customer shows unclear and broken memories, with flashes that feel strangely connected to the player. The sense that something is wrong in the library begins to grow stronger.

**Chapter 3 – The Truth of the Library**  
The player starts exploring the library more deeply and realizes there is no visible exit, as if the place exists outside of reality. Strange events begin happening, such as books shifting on their own and glowing without customers. Another customer arrives and reveals hints that the library is a space between life and death where lost souls gather to resolve unfinished stories. The player begins to suspect that nothing about this place is normal.

**Chapter 4 – The Flickering Book**  
The flickering book becomes more unstable and starts reacting whenever the player gets close to it. During one customer’s story, the player suddenly experiences flashes of memories that do not belong to any customer. These visions feel personal and emotional, suggesting a forgotten past connected to the library itself. The mystery of the flickering book becomes central to the player’s curiosity.

**Chapter 5 – The Player’s Story**  
The player finally opens the flickering book and is overwhelmed by fragments of their own memories. They see moments from their past, including a traumatic event like an accident that explains why they ended up in this place. It is revealed that the player is also one of the lost souls, and their story is just as unfinished as the customers they have been helping. The truth completely changes how they understand the library.

**Chapter 6 – The Final Choice**  
The library becomes silent as no more customers arrive, leaving only the player and the glowing books. The final book reveals the player’s fate and presents a final choice that will determine the ending of their story. They can choose to return to life, stay in the library as its new caretaker, or let go and move on peacefully. The ending changes depending on the choice, concluding their unfinished story.
